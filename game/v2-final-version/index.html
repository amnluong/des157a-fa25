Flowerfour Dice

How to play

  1. Each player will roll the dice the first round.
  2. After the first round each player will have the choice to roll or pass the dice to the opponent.
  3. If one of your die lands on 4, you will loose 4 points.
  4. If both of your dice lands on 4, you will loose 8 points.
  5. The first player who gets 50 points wins. Good luck!
