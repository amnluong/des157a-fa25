Website Form Usability Thoughts

10.14.25

When creating a form the less is more theory is significant since people do not like to spend a lot of time on it. Asking only relevant questions/ providing relevant information is key to success in having the users complete the form. To have users complete the form smoothly, designers have to style the form to make it easy for users to understand and complete fast...
